What is Spring Camp?

Spring Camp is a joyful festival where JVM-based server developers come together to share valuable technologies, experiences, and forge new connections.

Under the name 'developer', we are all colleagues. Just as our predecessors built greater harvests through the spirit of mutual aid, we aim to create a path of shared growth by exchanging knowledge with one another.

In the ever-changing waves of technology, we strive to share not just technical introductions, but 'valuable experiences'. Our goal is to deliver living stories of practical help—the know-how gained from countless trials and errors, and the wisdom of problem-solving.

We do the same work and share the same concerns, yet we are often strangers to each other. We will make this conference a precious moment where you can shake off awkwardness, have comfortable conversations, and build new connections.
